Support

How can we help?

Answers to common questions about creating songs, covers, and videos with Belter - plus how to reach a human.

🎵 Getting started

Open Create Music, describe a vibe, pick a mood, and tap Generate. Your finished song lands in your library with cover art, ready to play.

⚡ Credits & billing

Each plan refills credits monthly. Songs cost a flat amount; videos scale with song length. Manage your subscription in the App Store under your Apple ID.

🎬 AI Video help

Photo mode needs one clear selfie. Scene mode needs a selfie plus an example clip under 60 seconds - use your own or licensed footage.

🔒 Account & privacy

Sign in with Apple keeps things private and password-free. Your creations are tied to your account and stay yours.

Frequently asked questions

If your question isn't here, email us - we read every message.

My generation failed - was I charged?+

No. If a song, cover, or video fails before it finishes, the credits are automatically refunded to your balance. If you believe you were charged in error, contact us with the date and time and we'll make it right.

How long does a song take?+

Most songs finish in well under a minute. Videos take longer because they render frame-by-frame, and Scene videos run two steps (face-swap, then lip-sync), so they can take a few minutes.

Why won't my example video upload?+

Example clips must be under 60 seconds. Longer clips are rejected up front. Very large or unusual formats are automatically compressed before upload; if one still won't load, try a shorter or lower-resolution clip.

Can I change a song's cover art?+

Yes. Open the track in the player and tap the change-cover button to pick any photo from your library. The new art updates everywhere, including the lock screen.

Can I use my own voice?+

Yes - record a short voice sample once, and Belter can re-sing your songs in your voice while keeping the original instrumental.

Do I own and can I download what I make?+

Everything you create is saved to your library and is yours to keep. For Scene videos, please only use footage you own or are licensed to use.

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?+

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. On your iPhone, open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Belter, where you can change or cancel your plan at any time.

How do I delete my account or data?+

Email us from the address tied to your account and we'll permanently delete your account and associated creations. See the contact section below.

Still need a hand?

We typically reply within 1–2 business days.

Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026. This policy explains what data Belter collects, how we collect it, how we use it, and the third-party AI services we share it with to create your content.

What we collect and how

We collect: (a) account details from Sign in with Apple (name and email, provided by Apple on first sign-in); (b) the content you submit for generation — text prompts, lyrics, songs you create, voice recordings, and photos — which you provide directly in the app; and (c) usage and purchase information such as your credit balance and in-app purchases. We do not collect data from other sources and we do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

How we use your data

We use your data only to: generate the music, covers, and videos you request; operate your account, subscription, and credit balance; provide support; and keep the service secure. We do not sell your personal data and do not use it for advertising.

Third-party AI services we share data with

To produce your content, we send the material you provide to the following third-party AI processors, who use it solely to deliver their service to us:

  • ElevenLabs — receives your text prompts, lyrics, generated songs, and voice recordings, to generate music and convert vocals.
  • Replicate — receives your voice recordings, to re-sing tracks in a custom voice.
  • fal.ai — receives your photo, to create AI performance videos.
  • Supabase — hosts our backend, database, storage, and authentication.

These providers act as our processors: they process your data only to provide their service to us, do not use it for their own advertising, and are contractually required to protect it to a standard at least equivalent to this policy. We ask for your permission inside the app before sharing this data with them, and you can decline.

Voice & photo data

Voice recordings and photos are treated as sensitive. They are processed to create your requested output and are not used to train general-purpose AI models or for advertising.

Data retention & your rights

We keep your content and account data while your account is active. You can delete individual items in the app, and you can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account. Depending on your location you may also have rights to access, correct, or export your data — contact us to exercise them.
