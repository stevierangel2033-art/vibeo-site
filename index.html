FAQ

Good questions.

Do I need any musical skill?+

None at all. If you can describe a vibe in a sentence, you can make a song. Belter handles the writing, singing, mixing, and artwork.

How do credits work?+

Each creation uses credits - a song is a flat cost, and videos scale with the song's length since longer renders cost more to produce. Your plan refills credits every month, and you can top up any time.

What can I do in the AI Video feature?+

Two modes. Photo turns a single selfie into a video of you singing your song. Scene drops your face into an example clip and lip-syncs it to your track - great for recreating a performance with you in it.

Can I use my own voice?+

Yes. Record a short sample once and Belter can re-sing your songs in your own voice, keeping the original instrumental.

Do I own what I make?+

Everything you create stays in your library and is yours to keep and download. Use your own or properly licensed footage for Scene videos.

How do I get Belter?+

Belter is available now on the App Store. It's free to download and requires an iPhone running iOS 17 or later. Download it here and start creating in seconds.
